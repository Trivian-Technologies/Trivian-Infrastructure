## Overview

This repository is the home for **Trivian Technologies' broader infrastructure ecosystem** — the foundational layer that will support future products beyond Syzygy Rosetta.

Trivian Technologies is building governance and coordination infrastructure for the age of AI. Rosetta is the first product. The Trivian Infrastructure repository is where the next layer gets built.

---

## Planned Products

### Trivian Lattice Engine

Multi-agent coordination and governance at scale.

The Lattice Engine is designed for organizations running complex networks of autonomous AI agents that require cross-agent policy enforcement, coordination protocols, and governance at the network level — not just the individual agent level.

**Status:** Early design phase

---

### Gaian Interface

A human-AI relational protocol layer.

The Gaian Interface is designed for applications where the quality of human-AI interaction is a first-class concern — not just safety compliance. It establishes relational protocols that keep AI systems coherent, responsive, and aligned with human intent over extended interactions.

**Status:** Research and design phase

---

## Relationship to Syzygy Rosetta

Syzygy Rosetta is the **first product** in the Trivian infrastructure stack — the governance middleware layer.

Future products in this repository will extend the stack upward, adding coordination, relational, and ecosystem-level infrastructure on top of the governance foundation Rosetta establishes.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│       Gaian Interface               │  ← Relational layer (future)
├─────────────────────────────────────┤
│       Trivian Lattice Engine        │  ← Coordination layer (future)
├─────────────────────────────────────┤
│       Syzygy Rosetta                │  ← Governance layer (current)
├─────────────────────────────────────┤
│       AI Models / LLM Providers     │  ← Foundation
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```
